Live-Code Prototyping

AudioKit 2.0 introduces "Playgrounds" which use dynamic code-injection to allow you to develop instruments in real-time with immediate aural feedback.

Awesome High-Level Utilities

Built-in functionality for plotting waveforms, FFTs and property graphs, pre-made ready-to-use instruments, including a multi-track sampler, and many UI tools.

100+ Synthesizers and FX

Creates sounds with many synthesizers, physical models, and sample playback operations, and then process those sounds with effects, filters, delays, reverbs, and more.

Tons of Examples and Tests

Included and featured examples range from Hello World to fully developed audio apps. Our test suite enables us to keep improving with confidence everything is running as expected.

Powerful Sequencing

Sequencing comes in two flavors: phrases for the traditional changing notes and properties on a timeline, and sequence for triggering events that contain block of any code.

Sensible Defaults and Naming

Code with audio with audio metaphors - your Orchestra has Instruments that produce Notes. Plus, sensible defaults make things sound appropriate right of the box.
