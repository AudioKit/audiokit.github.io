initWithTable:time:scaledPitch:amplitude:

Instantiates the phase locked vocoder

- (instancetype)initWithTable:(AKControl *)table time:(AKAudio *)time scaledPitch:(AKControl *)scaledPitch amplitude:(AKControl *)amplitude

Parameters

table

Source signal table. Only mono audio files work.

time

Time position of current audio sample in secs. Table reading wraps around the ends of the table.

scaledPitch

Grain pitch scaling (1=normal pitch, < 1 lower, > 1 higher; negative, backwards)

amplitude

Amplitude scaling
