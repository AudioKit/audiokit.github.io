Overview

Applies portamento to a step-valued control signal. Applies portamento to a step-valued control signal. At each new step value, the output is low-pass filtered to move towards that value at a rate determined by halfTime. halfTime is the “half-time” of the function (in seconds), during which the curve will traverse half the distance towards the new value, then half as much again, etc., theoretically never reaching its asymptote.
