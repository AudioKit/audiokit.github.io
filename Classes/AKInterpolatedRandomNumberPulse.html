AKInterpolatedRandomNumberPulse.m

Overview

Generates a controlled random number series with interpolation between each new number. New random numbers are generated at a given frequency between zero and a maximum upper bound. In between random numbers, the value of this operation is linearly interpolated between the two numbers in time.
