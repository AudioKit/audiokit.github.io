initWithBodySize:frequency:pairedStringDetuning:pluckPosition:loopGain:amplitude:

Instantiates the mandolin

- (instancetype)initWithBodySize:(AKControl *)bodySize frequency:(AKControl *)frequency pairedStringDetuning:(AKControl *)pairedStringDetuning pluckPosition:(AKControl *)pluckPosition loopGain:(AKControl *)loopGain amplitude:(AKControl *)amplitude

Parameters

bodySize

The size of the body of the mandolin. Range 0 to 2.

frequency

Frequency of note played.

pairedStringDetuning

The proportional detuning between the two strings. Suggested range 0.9 to 1.

pluckPosition

The pluck position, in range 0 to 1. Suggest 0.4.

loopGain

The loop gain of the model, in the range 0.97 to 1.

amplitude

Amplitude of note.
