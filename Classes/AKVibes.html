Overview

Physical model related to the striking of a metal block.

Audio output is a tone related to the striking of a metal block as found in a vibraphone. The method is a physical model developed from Perry Cook.

Instantiates the vibes

- (instancetype)initWithFrequency:(AKControl *)frequency maximumDuration:(AKConstant *)maximumDuration stickHardness:(AKConstant *)stickHardness strikePosition:(AKConstant *)strikePosition amplitude:(AKControl *)amplitude tremoloShapeTable:(AKFTable *)tremoloShapeTable tremoloFrequency:(AKControl *)tremoloFrequency tremoloAmplitude:(AKControl *)tremoloAmplitude

Parameters

frequency

Frequency of note played.

maximumDuration

Time before end of note when damping is introduced

stickHardness

The hardness of the stick used in the strike. A range of 0 to 1 is used. 0.5 is a suitable value.

strikePosition

Where the block is hit, in the range 0 to 1.

amplitude

Amplitude of note.

tremoloShapeTable

Shape of tremolo, usually a sine table, created by a function

tremoloFrequency

Frequency of tremolo in Hertz. Suggested range is 0 to 12

tremoloAmplitude

Amplitude of the tremolo
