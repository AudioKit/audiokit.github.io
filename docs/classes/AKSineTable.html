Creates a sine table with an array of partial strengths

- (instancetype)initWithSize:(int)tableSize partialStrengths:(AKArray *)partialStrengthsArray

Parameters

tableSize

Number of points in the table. Must be a power of 2 or power-of-2 plus 1.

partialStrengthsArray

Relative strengths of the fixed harmonic partial numbers 1,2,3, etc. Partials not required should be given a strength of zero.
