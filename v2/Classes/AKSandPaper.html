AKSandPaper.m

Overview

Semi-physical model of a sandpaper sound. This is one of the PhISEM percussion opcodes. PhISEM (Physically Informed Stochastic Event Modeling) is an algorithmic approach for simulating collisions of multiple independent sound producing objects.

Properties
