AudioKit is built by audio designers, programmers, and musicians dedicated to the goal of making AudioKit the easiest possible audio platform to adopt.

If you run into any problems, go to GitHub and open a ticket. If you have any other questions about AudioKit, consulting, training, or sound design, send an email to audiokit@audiokit.io.
